# Privacy Policy for SanChallenges

**Last Updated: January 10, 2026**

SanChallenges ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how your information is collected, used, and disclosed by SanChallenges.

## 1. Information We Collect

*   **Account Information**: When you create an account, we may collect your name, email address, and profile picture.
*   **Challenge Data**: We collect information about the challenges you create, join, and your progress (check-ins).
*   **Device Information**: We may collect information about the device you use to access the app, including the hardware model, operating system, and unique device identifiers.

## 2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:
*   Provide, maintain, and improve our services.
*   Facilitate social interactions between users (e.g., rankings, nudges).
*   Send you technical notices, updates, and security alerts.

## 3. Data Sharing

We do not share your personal information with third parties except:
*   With other users as part of the app's social features (e.g., your display name and progress in a shared challenge).
*   To comply with legal obligations.

## 4. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your information from loss, theft, misuse, and unauthorized access.

## 5. Your Choices

You can update your account information or delete your account at any time within the app settings.
